Project

Skillet Restaurant Group is a local business in Seattle and we felt like we can make meaningful impact by improving their information management. This project will allow us to real-life skills related to business management and use our skills in database management and data analytics to improve Skillet's technology.

We chose our team project because our team members share an interest and skills in databases and data analytics. Our project combines these two interest areas because we will be designing a database and analytics dashboard. Working with a sponsor offers structure to our project and a challenge that we are excited for.

Research

Our project will require detailed data exploration of Skillet's organizational data + user interviews with our sponsors. These research methods will allow us to design thoughtful visualizations for the analytics dashboard to effectively serve the information needs of our client.

Conducting this data exploration will require more domain expertise of the restaurant management space, this is information we can seek from our sponsors in user interviews as well as doing some of our own independent research. In addition, field research will bring additional insights into the domain of restuarant management.
